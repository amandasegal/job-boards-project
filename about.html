Daenerys Targaryen, CEO

Daenerys is descended from hundreds of generations of conquerors. She brings her expertise in dragon training, winning over natives, and city operations to every aspect of our platform. Before starting TDT, she was the COO of Dothrak.ly, a food finding app for nomadic herders. In her spare time, she enjoys hair braiding and riding dragons.

Missandei, CTO

Missandei joins TDT as an expert in all things language related. Front-end, back-end, and in between, she knows them all. She makes TDT fly. When not advising the team in technical matters, you can find her doing volunteer work as a passionate advocate for the citizens of Meereen.

Tyrion Lannister, COO

Tyrion Lannister joined the team after a dramatic exit from LannisterMint, a premier personal finance application. He brings a lifetime of experience in manipulating day-to-day operations, and an in-depth knowledge of historical conquests to TDT. In his spare time he enjoys reading, fine wines, and travel.
